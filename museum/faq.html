Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do the museums listed in this website allows walk-in visitors?

Yes! All museums listed on this website accepts walk-in visitors. However, when visiting the museums in a large group (for around 20-30 persons), an advance booking may be required before visiting the museum.

Is there a dress code that I need to consider?

There is no dress code when visiting the museums listed. However, it is always best to dress approrioately to consider sensitivities of other visitors may have.

Can I take pictures inside the museum?

Yes, you are welcome to take a photograph of the permanent collection for your personal, nondistributional and noncommercial use. But take note that the use of flash is inappropriate and not permitted instide the premises.

Can I bring water to the museum?

Food, bottled water, and ballpoint pens are not permitted inside the museum. There is a designated place for food and beverage near the museum.

How long should I spend in the museum?

Well, it depends on which museum you are planning to visit. You could spend 2 hours inside the National Museums and the Mind Museum. However, Pinto Art Museum is a massive area, and if you are planning to see the entire place, allot 4 to 6 hours with breaks in between.

Want to Tell Us Something?

We entertain questions, suggestions and feedbacks. We definitely love to hear from you. If you want to us to help you with reservation to any of the museums listed on this website, feel free to send us an email, or you can call us.
